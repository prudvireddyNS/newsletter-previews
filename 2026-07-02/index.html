Unpatched Argo CD repo-server flaw can expose Kubernetes deployments Synacktiv disclosed an unpatched Argo CD repo-server flaw in its unauthenticated internal gRPC GenerateManifest service that can let an attacker reachable on the cluster network execute commands and potentially take over Kubernetes deployments. The attack abuses kustomize’s --helm-command option, was demonstrated against Argo CD v2.13.3, has no CVE or patch, and is mitigated by enabling Kubernetes NetworkPolicies around repo-server and Redis, especially for Helm installs where networkPolicy.create defaults to false.
